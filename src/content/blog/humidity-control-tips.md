## Why Humidity Matters

Maintaining the right humidity level is crucial for successful indoor farming. Both too high and too low humidity can cause problems...

## Target Humidity Levels by Growth Stage

- **Seedlings**: 70-80% RH
- **Vegetative**: 60-70% RH
- **Flowering**: 50-60% RH
